The Solution

An AI expert,
available in every aisle.

How It Works

Simple to use.
Powerful underneath.

1
Customer Interacts
Voice or text on the in-store totem. No app download, no registration required.
2
AI Researches in Real Time
LLMs query the live product catalog via RAG and agentic search for accurate, contextual answers.
3
Guidance is Delivered
The assistant compares options, answers follow-up questions, and guides the customer to a decision.
4
Data is Captured
Every interaction feeds the analytics dashboard — demand signals, feature priorities, inventory gaps.
