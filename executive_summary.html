in{Shop} is an AI shopping assistant for physical retail, deployed on tablet totems inside stores. Customers interact by voice or text to receive instant, deeply researched product guidance without an app, registration, or waiting for available staff.

The Problem

The store experience is broken.

High-consideration retail categories need expert guidance at the point of decision. Staff cannot always cover every product, use case, technical question, and upsell moment.

Expert guidance, live in-store.
