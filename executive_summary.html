Problem

Expert retail advice does not scale on the floor.

Physical retail loses sales when shoppers need expert advice and cannot get it in time. In electronics, appliances, hardware, DIY, and automotive, staff cannot consistently answer technical questions, comparisons, or use-case-specific needs.

Solution

Tilora brings instant product guidance to the shelf.

Tilora is an in-store AI assistant deployed on tablet totems. Shoppers use voice or text to ask questions, compare products, and get recommendations without downloading an app. Tilora uses live catalogue and ERP data plus structured product knowledge, and it supports staff rather than replacing them.

Market & Wedge

Croatia first, then regional expansion across CEE.

Tilora enters through high-consideration retail where advice quality affects conversion. The plan is to prove ROI in Croatia, expand into Slovenia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, and Serbia, and build toward a broader CEE retail platform.

  • Beachhead: Croatia, estimated EUR 2M initial market
  • Expansion: Slovenia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Serbia
  • Long-term: Broader CEE retail platform
  • Target verticals: Consumer electronics, hardware and DIY, home appliances, automotive showrooms, and specialised retail.

Business Model

Subscription revenue with an additional marketing layer.

Tilora is sold as a per-device subscription, with session capacity, support, and analytics scaled by retailer size. A second revenue layer can come from supplier-funded product placement inside the guidance flow.
