Tilora Executive Summary · 2026
AI Shopping Assistant for Physical Retail

AI Expert in Every Aisle.

Tilora is an in-store AI assistant deployed on tablet totems for physical retail. Customers interact by voice or text to receive instant product guidance, comparisons, and answers grounded in live catalogue data without downloading an app or waiting for available staff.

Why Now
Retail is ready for AI-assisted buying.

The gap is clear: stores need an always-available expert layer that improves conversion, captures unmet demand, and supports premium product positioning inside physical retail.

No app, no waiting Customers get immediate product guidance at the point of decision.
Live retailer context Answers draw on catalogue data, ERP integrations, and structured product knowledge.
Commercial upside Retailers gain analytics, inventory insight, and a new in-store marketing surface.

The Problem

The store experience is broken.

High-consideration categories like electronics, appliances, and automotive require expert guidance at the point of decision. Retail staff cannot consistently cover every use case, technical question, and upsell opportunity, which leads directly to lost sales and abandoned intent.

Customer Experience

"I need a laptop for SolidWorks"

Customers need tailored answers that floor staff cannot always provide in real time.

Retail Intelligence

"We don't have that right now"

Retailers lack a clean signal on unmet demand, missing inventory, and fast-changing trends.

Marketing

"This TV has AI features"

Premium features are often poorly framed, which weakens conversion and upsell outcomes.

The Solution

An AI expert, available in every aisle.

Tilora combines voice and text interaction, LLMs, retrieval-augmented generation, agentic catalogue research, and ERP connectivity to deliver contextual answers based on current retailer data.

Input

Voice & Text

Customers switch naturally between voice and text in the same session.

Engine

Deep Research

Product comparisons and follow-up answers are grounded in live catalogue context.

Systems

ERP Integration
